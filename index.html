Welcome to Sona Beauty Salon

Sona Beauty Salon is your neighborhood salon for all your beauty needs, located conveniently at 65 South Washington Ave in Bergenfield, NJ. We offer a variety of services from hair removal to facials.

We use various South Asian and western techniques such as Threading and Waxing to remove any unwanted hair. Our expert staff specializes in the ancient art of threading. Using a thread we can gently and precisely shape your eyebrows, to give you a look that will make you feel rejuvenated. To remove unwanted hair from other parts of body, we use waxing techniques.

We also offer facials and hair styling services. Customer satisfaction is our top priority. Our staff will not only take care of all your beauty needs but will educate you on how to better take care of yourself. Call us today at (201) 387 1954 for any questions or to make an appointment. We welcome Walk-Ins for threading services.
